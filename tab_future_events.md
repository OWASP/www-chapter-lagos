### Next Meeting/Event(s)

This event will be free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP and is open to anyone interested in application security and cyber security.

Register to attend this event at [meetup](https://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Lagos-Meetup-Group/events/273060702/) or [eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-talks-tickets-119763615197?aff=ebdssbonlinesearch).
